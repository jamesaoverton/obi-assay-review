  label: PCR-SSCP assay
  alternative term: polymerase chain reaction-single strand conformation polymorphism assay
  definition: a PCR-SSCP assay is an assay that identifies DNA sequence variation (mutation, deletion, insertions) using gel electrophoresis technique and denaturating conditions on target DNA sequences amplified using polymerase chain reaction procedure.
  definition source: PMID: 18219595.Mol Biotechnol. 2008 Feb;38(2):155-63.PCR-SSCP: a method for the molecular analysis of genetic diseases.
