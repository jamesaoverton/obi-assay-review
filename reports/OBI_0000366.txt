label: metabolite profiling
alternative term: metabolite assay
definition: metabolite profiling is a process which aims at detecting and identifying chemical entities resulting from biochemical and cellular metabolism
definition source: OBI
example of usage: Metabolite profiling of human colon carcinoma - deregulation of TCA cycle and amino acid turnover. Mol Cancer. 2008 Sep 18;7(1):72. PMID: 18799019
term editor: Philippe Rocca-Serra
