  label: copy number variation profiling
  alternative term: CNV analysis
  definition: copy number variation profiling is a process which aims to provide information about lost or amplified genomic regions of DNA by comparing genomic DNA originated from tissues from same or different individuals using specific techniques such as CGH, array CGH, SNP genotyping.
